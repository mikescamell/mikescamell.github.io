I love to stuff my face and that's what Sundays are for

I enjoy performing food challenges.

Even if they are just food challenges set by myself at home. My girlfriend gets the pleasure of watching these great feats (and also experiences the results after). This time I decided to time lapse my demolishing of a double decker 14 inch pizza. It went quite well, and also helped me quickly digest the 400 grams of white rice and 6 eggs that I had for breakfast. Enjoy.
